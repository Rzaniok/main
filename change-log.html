Version 1.3.0 [GAMES UPDATE]

This update is mostly focused on games!

What did we add?

• Few new commands: !guess & !yordle & !games
• Two new global leaderboards! POINTS & YORDLE's WINS.

Game !guess is all about guessing the champion's name and skill based on a picture or emotes!

Game !yordle is about guessing the name of the character in the same way as the game WORDLE, i.e. you guess the characters and show you which letters you hit.

Some other patch-notes:
• Based on your suggestions we added WIN-STREAK to minigames.
• Added EXTRA POINTS for currect guesstes when your win streak if high.

Some bot changes:
• Definitely fixed the MOST PLAYED WITH error.
• Changed display of few items (adjusted to new patch)
• Changed the !top icon - now its only displaying league of legends data.
